Mazda MX-5 Miata NB

The NB is the second generation of the Mazda MX-5 Miata.
It replaced the pop-up headlights with fixed headlights and arrived for the 1998 model year.

Key facts for this test document:
- Generation code: NB
- Production era: 1998 to 2005
- Signature change: fixed headlights instead of pop-up headlights
- Special performance version: Mazdaspeed Miata
- Mazdaspeed Miata years: 2004 and 2005

Simple summary:
If a question mentions fixed headlights or the factory turbo Mazdaspeed Miata,
the answer should come from this NB document.
